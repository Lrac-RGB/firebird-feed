How this feed is built

Phoenixville Feed gathers what's posted on Phoenixville's official borough calendar and the town's main venues and organizers, then lays it out in one chronological view you can filter by source. Listings reflect what those sources have published; always tap through to confirm dates, times and tickets before you head out.

Got something to add? The borough takes public submissions through the Submit Event form on its calendar, and most venues list their own shows directly.

Compiled from publicly posted listings · Times and dates subject to change by the organizer · Not affiliated with the Borough of Phoenixville.
